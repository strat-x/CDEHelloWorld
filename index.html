Welcome to the CDE Hello World plugin page.

This plugin integrates with CA RA CDE and welcomes any new release that is marked as release candidate and pushed through the pipeline

Options

the plugin supports a "welcome on board of our CD pipeline" and "thanks for allowing us to guide you through the CD pipeline today".
